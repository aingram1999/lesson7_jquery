Chocolate chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cup flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts(optional)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350.

Beat butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla until light and fluffy.

Mix in flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.

Stir until well blended.

Mix in the chocolate chips and walnuts.

Drop by rounded teaspoons onto a cookie sheet.

Bake for 8-10 minutes in preheated oven.

Let cool before eating.
